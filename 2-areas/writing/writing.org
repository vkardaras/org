#+title: Writing

* Types of Writing

|                   | Goal                                        | Audience                          | Length | Tonality                                   | Context                 |
|-------------------+---------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------+--------+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------|
| Technical Writing | helps to understand and use technology      | users, team members, stakeholders | long   | neutral                                    | wikis, style guides etc |
| UX Writing        | helps users to use a product                | users                             | short  | brand-driven                               | in product              |
| Copywriting       | grabs attention, engages, persuades         | potential customers               | short  | provocative, amusing, humorous, convincing | ads, marketing channels |
| Content Writing   | informs, creates awareness, grabs attention | interested readers                | long   | neutral (+ personal voice of author)       | blogs, media outlets    |
